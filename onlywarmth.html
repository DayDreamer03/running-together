What We Do

Let’s be honest: the world has gone crazy... and so have we. We’re Simon and Victor, 19 and 22, two brothers on a mission to run the Paris 2025 Marathon. Our goal is simple - to raise €10 for every kilometer we run. We all had our share of hospital visits. We know how hard it can be for patients, staff and helpers.

Why You Matter

Public hospitals are the last place of hope for so many. And guess what? They’re struggling. The staff? They’re exhausted, overworked, and underappreciated. Your donation isn't just cash – it's a lifeline. 100% of the funds collected will go straight to the French Fondation des hopitaux and the German Aktions Bündnis Gesundheit, in other words: improving conditions, buying equipment, and supporting the well-being of our docs.

Here’s the deal: our marathon and your donation isn't going to change the game. We're a drop in an ocean of debt. But it's a simple gesture to thank and give back to the people who show up for us every single day.
